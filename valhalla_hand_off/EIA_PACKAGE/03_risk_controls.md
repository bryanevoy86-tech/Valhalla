# Risk Controls & Safeguards

Valhalla incorporates multiple layers of protection:

## Operational Controls

- **Sandbox isolation** before live operation
- **DRY-RUN enforcement** for all irreversible actions
- **Human approval gates** at critical decision points
- **Volume and exposure caps** per transaction
- **Conservative capital allocation** rules

## Financial Controls

- Low personal extraction (10% maximum)
- Majority reinvestment for sustainability
- Clear fund separation
- No leverage dependency
- Monthly reconciliation and review

## Technical Controls

- Automated lead scoring and validation
- Deal structure templates
- Audit trails for all actions
- Real-time monitoring and alerts
- Graceful degradation in error states

---

## Purpose

These controls are designed to:
- Prevent overextension
- Protect capital
- Ensure survivability
- Enable learning and iteration
- Maintain stakeholder confidence
