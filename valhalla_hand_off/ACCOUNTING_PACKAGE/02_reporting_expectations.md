# Reporting Expectations

## Monthly Reporting

- Summary of transactions
- Fund flow breakdown
- Income categorization
- Expense categorization
- Period-over-period comparison

## Record Keeping

- Transaction-level records maintained
- Deal documentation archived
- Income sources clearly identified
- Expenses logically categorized

## Categories

**Income:**
- Wholesale fees
- Assignment fees
- Transactional spreads
- Investment returns

**Expenses:**
- Lead acquisition
- Deal analysis
- Operational costs
- Technology/systems

---

## No Strategic Input Required

Accounting's role is purely execution:
- Categorize transactions
- Maintain records
- Generate reports
- Flag anomalies

No strategic decision-making is required from accounting. All strategy is determined by operations leadership.
