# Playbook: Buyer Dry-Up

## Early signals
- Time-to-exit rising
- Buyer response rates falling
- Retrades increasing

## Immediate actions
- Tighten buyer reliability scoring
- Expand buyer intake (no outbound automation until approved)
- Improve deal packet clarity
- Reduce deal volume temporarily to protect margin

## Do NOT do
- Do not drop standards to force exits
- Do not panic-scale outbound

## Recovery success criteria
- Response rate normalizes
- Exit time stabilizes
- Retrades decrease
