# Playbook: Legal Delays / Friction

## Early signals
- Contract review queue expanding
- Closing timelines slipping
- Increased exceptions and special cases

## Immediate actions
- Standardize templates
- Batch questions for counsel (reduce back-and-forth)
- Keep a clean deal packet for legal handoff
- Reduce complexity of deal types temporarily

## Do NOT do
- Do not invent legal workarounds under pressure
- Do not proceed without counsel on flagged cases

## Recovery success criteria
- Average review time returns to baseline
- Exceptions drop
