# DECISION LOG

Complete record of all major operator decisions, rationale, and outcomes.

## Format

Use this structure for every major decision:

---

### Decision [#]

**Decision:** (What was decided?)  
**Date:** (When was the decision made?)  

**Context:**  
(What was the situation? What prompted this decision?)

**Options Considered:**  
(What choices existed?)

**Heimdall Advice:**  
(What did Heimdall recommend? Any challenges raised?)

**Operator Choice:**  
(What did the operator decide and why?)

**Rationale:**  
(Operator's reasoning in their own words)

**Date Implemented:**  
**Outcome (completed later):**  
(How did it actually turn out? Did it work as expected?)

---
