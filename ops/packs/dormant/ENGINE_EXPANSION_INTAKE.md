# Engine Expansion Intake (Sandbox-First)

Purpose: Add new revenue engines without chaos.

## Intake Questions
1) What is the engine?
2) What is the smallest test?
3) What is the expected return?
4) What are the risks?
5) What does success look like?
6) What data is required?
7) What must be gated?
8) What gets certified before going live?

## Output
- A 3-phase sandbox plan
- Metrics to certify it
- Guardrails (caps, approvals, kill switch)
- Decision: Park / Continue / Promote
