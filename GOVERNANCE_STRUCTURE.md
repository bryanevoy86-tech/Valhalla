## 1. GOVERNANCE LAYER

**Where the rules live. This is locked.**

### [doctrine.md](governance/doctrine.md)
The founding principles of the system. Non-negotiable.

- Infinite scale, finite risk
- System-first priority
- Sandbox-first for all engines
- Reversibility for all irreversible actions

**Read this first. Everything else flows from here.**

### [extraction_policy.md](governance/extraction_policy.md)
Capital allocation locked for 5-year compounding window.

- Minimum extraction: 10% (never reduced)
- Duration: 5+ years (no increases during window)
- Post-compounding review optional, reversible

**This is frozen. No modifications until Phase 4+ review.**

### [heimdall_role.md](governance/heimdall_role.md)
Operator + Heimdall relationship model.

- Heimdall: Challenge authority (can advise against)
- Operator: Final authority (always decides)
- Neither blocks the other

**Heimdall succeeds when operator makes better-informed decisions.**

## 2. ENGINE LIFECYCLE

**How engines move from idea to production.**

### [ENGINE_REGISTRY.md](engines/ENGINE_REGISTRY.md)
Complete inventory of all engines.

- Status of each engine (candidate, testing, active)
- Lifecycle stages
- Current: Valhalla Core (Phase 3 certified)
- Candidates: Storage cleanouts, landscaping, arbitrage

**This is the source of truth for what's running and what's pending.**

### Engine Certification Templates

All new engines follow this path:

**STAGE 0: [ENGINE_INTAKE.md](engines/templates/ENGINE_INTAKE.md)**
- Capture the idea
- Document assumptions
- Go/no-go checklist

**STAGE 1: [ENGINE_SOLO_SANDBOX_CERT.md](engines/templates/ENGINE_SOLO_SANDBOX_CERT.md)**
- Test in isolation (DRY_RUN=1)
- Prove stability
- Document results

**STAGE 2: [ENGINE_INTEGRATED_SANDBOX_CERT.md](engines/templates/ENGINE_INTEGRATED_SANDBOX_CERT.md)**
- Test alongside other engines
- Prove it doesn't break anything
- Establish resource throttles if needed

**STAGE 3: [ENGINE_READY_CERT.md](engines/templates/ENGINE_READY_CERT.md)**
- Approval gate
- Dormant but safe
- Phase 4 activation ready

## 3. ADVISORY LAYER

**Strategic decision frameworks. Informational, not prescriptive.**

### [BUSINESS_GPS.md](advisory/BUSINESS_GPS.md)
Route-planning framework.

```
Operator: "I want to reach X"
Heimdall: "Here are all routes to X"
Operator: Chooses route
Heimdall: Executes chosen route
```

Every route includes:
- Steps
- Timeline
- Risks
- Trade-offs
- Reversibility

### [CHALLENGE_REPORT.md](advisory/CHALLENGE_REPORT.md)
When Heimdall recommends against a proposed action.

- What's the concern?
- What are alternatives?
- What happens if you proceed anyway?
- Operator override documented

**Challenges surface risks, not block progress.**

### [DECISION_LOG.md](advisory/DECISION_LOG.md)
Complete record of all major operator decisions.

- What was decided
- Why (operator's reasoning)
- Heimdall's advice
- Outcome (filled in later)

**System memory. Review periodically to identify patterns.**
