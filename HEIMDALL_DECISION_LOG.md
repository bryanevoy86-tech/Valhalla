# HEIMDALL DECISION LOG

Purpose: Maintain a simple, auditable record of
major system decisions and approvals.

---

## Required Entries
Log only decisions that affect:
- Spend
- Scope
- Risk
- Activation state
- Tool adoption

---

## Entry Format

```
Date:
Decision Type: (Tool / Scope / Engine / Policy)
Proposal Summary:
Primary Recommendation:
Fallback Option:
Cost Impact:
Survival Gate Status:
Decision: (Approved / Rejected / Deferred)
Notes:
```

---

## Why This Exists
- Protects future-you from past-you
- Creates clarity under pressure
- Demonstrates governance to EI or partners
- Prevents silent drift

---

## Rule
If it's not logged, it didn't happen.

---

## Archive
Keep this log in version control.
Update at each major decision point.
